      FAQ – Bathtub & Tile Reglazing

      What is reglazing and how is it different from replacement?

      Reglazing is the professional refinishing of bathtubs, tiles, or sinks without removing them. Replacement means a full remodel with demolition, higher costs, and more time. Reglazing is faster and more cost-effective if the base surface is in fair condition.

      How much does it cost?

      Prices depend on the type, size, and condition of the surface. Check our Pricing page for starting rates. For an exact estimate, please send us photos or request an on-site evaluation.

      How long does the job take and when can I use my bathtub?

      Most reglazing projects are completed in just a few hours. Surfaces are usually ready to use within 24 hours, depending on the material and room conditions.

      Is there any smell during the process? Is it safe?

      Some odor may occur during application, but we use proper ventilation and follow safety protocols. Once the coating cures and the bathroom is aired out, the odor disappears.

      How long does the finish last and is there a warranty?

      The lifespan depends on use and care, but reglazed surfaces can last for years. We provide a written warranty, and our technician explains the terms before the job begins.

      Can you refinish tiles, sinks, and shower pans?

      Yes, we reglaze bathtubs, tiles, sinks, countertops, and shower pans. Just send us a photo and we will confirm feasibility and preparation steps.

      Why do chips or peeling sometimes appear?

      The main reasons are harsh cleaning products, mechanical damage, or improper care. We provide aftercare instructions to help you avoid these issues.

      What colors are available?

      Standard finish is white and light shades, but custom colors can be arranged upon request. We confirm the palette before starting the project.

      How should I prepare my bathroom before the technician arrives?

      Please clear the work area, remove items from the tub edges, and ensure access to ventilation and electricity. Let us know if there are leaks or loose fixtures.

      How should I care for the reglazed surface?

      Use mild, non-abrasive cleaners and soft sponges. Avoid bleach, harsh acids, or metal scrubbers. Do not drop heavy objects or use suction cups with sharp edges.

      What areas do you serve?

      We provide reglazing services across New York City and New Jersey, including Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, Staten Island, the Bronx, and nearby NJ cities.

      How can I get an estimate?

      Fill out the form on our Contact page or send us a photo with a short description. We will reply with pricing and the nearest available date.
